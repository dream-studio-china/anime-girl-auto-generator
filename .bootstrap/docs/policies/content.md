# Content Policy

## Core Rules

All content must be legal, non-deceptive, and safe for the intended audience. The agent should be conservative when content is ambiguous.

## AI Disclosure

Posts should not imply that generated images are real people, real events, or photographs. Prefer wording such as:
- `Generated with ComfyUI`
- `AI illustration`
- `AI-generated anime artwork`

## Adult and Edge Content

Adult or edge content includes:
- nudity or partial nudity
- lingerie, underwear, bikini, swimsuit, or focus on sexualized body parts
- explicit or implied sexual behavior
- hentai or pornographic framing
- text intended to cause sexual arousal

If present:
- Show an adult-content warning in the review card.
- Require user confirmation that X sensitive/adult media settings are correct.
- Publish only with `--adult-content`.
- Do not use adult content in avatars, banners, or other high-visibility account surfaces.

## Minors and Ambiguous Age

Never publish sexualized content involving minors or characters that could reasonably be interpreted as minors.

Avoid combining youth-coded terms with sexualized styling, including:
- child, kid, loli, teen, schoolgirl, underage
- petite/young-looking framing plus lingerie, nudity, erotic poses, or sexual captions

If age is ambiguous and the image is sexualized, block publishing and ask for a safer generation.

## Copyright and Identity

Avoid:
- copying living artists' work too closely
- using protected characters or franchises in a way that implies official affiliation
- using real-person names, likenesses, or identities without permission
- scraping prompts or images and reproducing them without transformation or attribution

Prefer original character descriptions and broad style language.

## Hashtag Rules

Use 3-6 hashtags per post by default.

Allowed:
- Relevant content tags: `#animegirl`, `#AIart`
- Relevant style tags: `#cyberpunk`, `#kimono`, `#kawaii`
- Relevant community tags: `#AIイラスト`, `#stablediffusion`

Avoid:
- unrelated trending tags
- excessive tag stuffing
- repeating identical hashtag sets across many posts
- tags implying real-world events or identities when the image is fictional

## Captions

Captions should be concise and not spammy.

Good captions:
- describe mood, character, style, or workflow
- disclose AI/ComfyUI generation
- ask one natural engagement question when appropriate

Avoid captions that:
- aggressively demand likes, follows, reposts, or replies
- misrepresent the image as real
- imply non-consensual or underage sexual content
- use misleading trend bait

## Trend Use

Trend research should guide creative direction, not manipulate distribution. Only use trend-derived tags if the image directly matches the topic.
